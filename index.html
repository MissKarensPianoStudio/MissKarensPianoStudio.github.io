Miss Karen's Piano Studio

  • Classically trained pianist with over 40 years of teaching experience.
  • Bachelor of Music degree from Biola University.
  • Active member of the Music Teacher's Association of California.

Each student receives private instruction. Lessons are once per week at either 30, 45 minutes or 1 hour.

The ultimate goal is for the student to be able to read music well and to perform musically and expressively.

All students participate in group recitals.

Students of all ages are accepted. Lessons are available on a direct pay basis. Miss Karen is also a vendor for the following charter schools:

  • Granite Mountain
  • Sage Oak
  • Sky Mountain
